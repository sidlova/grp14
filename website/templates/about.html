About Bussary Boost

Bussary Boost is a financial aid search engine that helps students find scholarships, grants,and other forms of financial assistance. Our mission is to make financial aid more accessible and easier to navigate for students and families.

With Bussary Boost, students can search for financial aid opportunities by keyword, location, and eligibility criteria. We also provide resources and tools to help students navigate the financial aid process, including a financial aid calculator, a glossary of financial aid terms, and a guide to the FAFSA.

Bussary Boost is a free service, and we do not charge students or financial aid providers to use our platform. We are funded through advertising and sponsorships.

If you have any questions or feedback, please contact us.
